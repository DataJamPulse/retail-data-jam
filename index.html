Frequently Asked Questions

How does JamBox count people without cameras?

JamBox detects the signals that mobile phones naturally emit when searching for WiFi networks. It counts these signals anonymously without collecting any personal information. No cameras, no facial recognition, no tracking individuals.

Is this legal and GDPR compliant?

Yes. JamBox counts anonymized signals only. We don't collect personal data, don't identify individuals, and don't track anyone. It's been designed from the ground up to respect privacy while giving you the insights you need.

How hard is it to set up?

If you can plug in a lamp and connect a phone to WiFi, you can set up JamBox. It takes about 5 minutes. No drilling, no wiring, no IT support needed.

What kind of businesses use this?

Any business that wants to understand their customer traffic: shops, cafes, restaurants, salons, gyms, galleries, pop-ups, and more. If people walk past or through your business, JamBox can count them.

How accurate is it?

Very accurate for understanding patterns. JamBox counts mobile devices, and since most people carry phones, it gives you a reliable picture of foot traffic. It's designed to show you trends and patterns, not exact headcounts.

Can I try it before committing?

Yes! Get in touch and we'll set up a trial so you can see your own footfall data before making any decisions.

What does it cost?

JamBox is designed to be affordable for small businesses. Contact us for current pricing and see if there's a package that fits your needs.

Do I need multiple JamBoxes for my business?

Most small shops, cafes, and salons only need one JamBox. Larger premises or businesses wanting to measure different areas might benefit from multiple units. We'll help you figure out what's right for your space.
