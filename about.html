Bringing Footfall Data to Every Business

We believe every high street business deserves to understand their customer traffic. Not just the big chains. Everyone.

Our Mission

Data Jam Retail exists to give small and medium businesses the same insights that big retailers take for granted.

Walk into any major chain store and they know exactly how many people visit each hour. They use that data to schedule staff, plan promotions, and make smarter decisions. But for independent shops, cafes, and high street businesses? Most are flying blind.

That felt wrong to us. So we built JamBox—a simple, affordable device that counts customer traffic automatically. No cameras, no personal data, no complex setup. Just real numbers that help you run your business better.

Our goal is simple: make footfall analytics accessible to every business, regardless of size or budget.

Our Values

Designed for SMBs

Honest Data

Why Footfall Data Matters

The businesses that thrive on the high street are the ones that understand their customers.

Every major retailer tracks footfall religiously. They know their peak hours, their quiet periods, and how external factors like weather and local events affect their traffic. That data shapes everything from staffing to marketing to lease negotiations.

Until now, this kind of insight has been out of reach for smaller businesses. The technology was either too expensive, too complex, or too invasive (think cameras and facial recognition).

Data Jam Retail changes that. We've made footfall counting:

  • Affordable — priced for independent businesses
  • Simple — setup takes minutes, not days
  • Private — no cameras, no personal data
  • Actionable — clear data you can actually use
